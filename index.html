Short Bio
I'm currently an undergraduate student at School of Electronic and Information Engineering, Xi'an Jiaotong University (XJTU). I am a research assistant supervised by Prof. Dexing Zhong. I am currently also a student intern at Computational Biology Department in Carneige Mellon University (CMU). At XJTU and CMU, my research interest would be machine learning algorithms and their applications on biomedical images and biometrics (i.e. face, fingerprint and palmprint). I am especially interested in transfer learning based few-shot learning, meta learning, active learning and related applications. I am searching for Ph.D position in Computer Vision and Machine Learning.
