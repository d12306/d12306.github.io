Short Bio
I'm currently an undergraduate student at School of Electronic and Information Engineering, Xi'an Jiaotong University (XJTU). I am a research assistant supervised by Prof. Dexing Zhong. I am currently also a student intern at Computational Biology Department and Machine Learning Department in Carneige Mellon University (CMU). At XJTU and CMU, my research interest would be machine learning algorithms and their applications on biomedical images and biometrics (i.e. face, fingerprint and palmprint). More Recently, I shift my interest to more general topics, such as transfer learning based few-shot learning, meta learning, active learning, adversarail issues and related applications in network embedding. I will become a visiting student at Multimedia Lab at the Chinese University of Hong Kong (CUHK) at my senior year. I am searching for Ph.D position in Computer Vision and Machine Learning.
